# Milestone Gate Framework

The program operated under defined milestone gates tied to critical path sequencing.

---

## Gate 1 — Construction Completion
Criteria:
- Remodel finished
- Inspection-ready state achieved

Enables:
- Departmental sign-off process

---

## Gate 2 — Departmental Compliance Sign-Off
Criteria:
- Building inspection passed
- Fire inspection cleared
- Agency documentation validated

Enables:
- Liquor permit approval

---

## Gate 3 — Liquor Permit Issuance (Critical Path Gate)
Criteria:
- All agency approvals complete
- License formally issued

Enables:
- Hiring activation
- Inventory acquisition
- Training launch
- Operational ramp

---

## Gate 4 — Operational Readiness
Criteria:
- Staff trained
- Systems tested
- Inventory stocked
- Scheduling built

Enables:
- Go-live

---

## Gate 5 — Revenue Stabilization
Criteria:
- Opening executed
- Revenue validation achieved
- Governance loop active

Enables:
- Scaling phase
