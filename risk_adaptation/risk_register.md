# Program Risk Register

The remodel and launch program carried structural risk due to regulatory dependency and capital exposure.

Below is the simplified risk tracking framework used during execution.

---

## Risk Register Table

| Risk ID | Risk Description | Impact Level | Likelihood | Mitigation Strategy | Status |
|----------|-----------------|--------------|------------|--------------------|--------|
| R1 | Liquor permit delay | Critical | High | Freeze downstream streams, preserve liquidity | Occurred |
| R2 | Construction overrun | High | Medium | Track vendor milestones weekly | Managed |
| R3 | Regulatory re-compliance costs | Medium | Medium | Maintain contingency buffer | Occurred |
| R4 | Premature hiring burn | Critical | High | Gate hiring behind permit approval | Prevented |
| R5 | Inventory spoilage before opening | High | Medium | Delay inventory acquisition | Prevented |
| R6 | Training inefficiency during compression | Medium | Medium | Daily coordination and review | Managed |

---

## Risk Classification Logic

Risks were evaluated based on:

- Liquidity impact
- Revenue activation delay
- Compounding cost exposure
- Reversibility of commitment

Critical risks were those that directly threatened capital runway.
